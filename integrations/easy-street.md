# Easy Street Offers — Partner Context

**Partner type:** Acquisition partner
**Legal entity:** DNMN Investments LLC
**Relationship:** ESO sources and holds assignable contracts; Opendoor is the ultimate buyer

---

## What Easy Street Offers Does

Easy Street Offers (ESO) is an off-market property acquisition company. They:
- Identify sellers who want a fast, certain sale
- Negotiate purchase contracts with those sellers
- Open title and manage the earnest money
- Assign the contract to Opendoor as the final buyer (≤7 days before COE)

Their business model is built on assignment — they never intend to be the final buyer.
