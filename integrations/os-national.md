# OS National — Integration Overview

**Partner type:** Title and escrow company
**Role in programs:** Primary title company for ESO transactions and general Opendoor closings

---

## What OS National Does

OS National (OSN) handles title and escrow services for Opendoor transactions. In the context of agent partnership programs, they are most directly involved in:

- ESO pilot transactions (title opening, EMD management, closing coordination)
- General partner transaction closings where OSN is selected as title

## ESO-Specific Role

In ESO transactions, OS National:
1. Receives the opening escrow package from ESO
2. Holds the earnest money deposit (EMD) from ESO
3. Manages title search and any title issues
4. Holds HOA documents when applicable (open question: how long?)
5. Coordinates closing with all parties

## Escalation

For title issues on ESO transactions:
1. Email clientservices@osnational.com with property address and specific issue

3. Loop in Acquisition Home Advisor (Jackson)
4. For persistent issues → Program Manager (Tara) escalation
