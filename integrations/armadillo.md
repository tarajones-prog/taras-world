## What Armadillo Does

Armadillo provides home warranty coverage — a product that protects buyers (and sometimes sellers) against covered repair costs for home systems and appliances after closing.

In the context of Opendoor's agent partnership programs, Armadillo may be:
- Offered to sellers or buyers as part of the transaction
- Bundled with certain Opendoor products
- Referenced in agent communications as a value-add
