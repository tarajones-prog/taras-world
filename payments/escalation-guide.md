## Escalation Path: Partner Payment Not Received

**Step 1:** Check the relevant payment tracker
- "Agent Led Offers Payments 2026" — for ALO
- "Monthly Partner Payments Tracker" — for referrals
- "Check Request Form v.2 (Responses)" — for original requests

**Step 2:** Check Ramp for payment status
- Look up the PO by payee name or date
- Confirm if it was approved, sent, and cleared

**Step 3:** Verify the broker's payment information
- Correct entity name?
- Correct address?
- Any returned payments in Ramp history?

**Step 4:** Contact the broker
- "We see a payment was processed on [date] — can you confirm if you received it? If not, we'll reissue."
- If returned: get updated address → reissue in Ramp

**Step 5:** If still unresolved → escalate to Melissa Giersz (ALO/referral) or Tara for program-level escalation

---

## Escalation Path: Unattributed Incoming Payment

When Opendoor receives a payment from a partner with no clear property address:

1. Search "Monthly Partner Payments Tracker" for any expected payments from that entity
2. Search "Check Request Form v.2" for pending requests from that entity
3. Contact the sending entity: "We received a payment from [entity] — can you confirm which property this relates to?"
4. Hold in reconciliation until confirmed
5. Document in tracker: "Address pending from [entity]"
