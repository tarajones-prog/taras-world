## Universal Payment Rules

These rules apply across all payment types:

1. **Always include the property address in the memo.** Payments without address context are nearly impossible to reconcile later. (Learned the hard way from an unresolved October payment.)

2. **Pay to the legal entity, not the individual.** Confirm the broker/partner entity name before creating any Ramp PO.

3. **Track before you pay.** Every payment should be recorded in the relevant tracking sheet before it's processed.

4. **No duplicate payments.** Cross-check the tracker before processing.

5. **Payment is after closing.** No program pays at contract — all payments are post-close.

## Payment Process by Tool

### Ramp
Used for most ALO, referral, and commission payments.
Full process: [ramp-process.md](ramp-process.md)

### Check / Legacy
Some older referral payments were issued as checks. This is being phased out in favor of Ramp.

### FSS (Final Settlement Statement)
ESO assignment fees are recorded on the FSS at closing — not a separate Ramp PO.
