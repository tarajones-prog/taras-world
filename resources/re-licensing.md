## Why This Matters for Program Work

Active real estate licenses are sometimes required or helpful when:
- Reviewing agent-facing communications and partner agreements
- Understanding regulatory context for program design
- Participating in transactions where a licensed PM adds value

---

## CE Resources

- Each state has its own Department of Real Estate (DRE) or equivalent licensing board
- CE providers vary by state — check your current provider or find an approved provider on the state licensing board website
- Many CE courses are available online and can be completed at your own pace
