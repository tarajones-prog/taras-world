## Severity Guide

| Severity | Description | Response Time |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| CRITICAL | Deal at risk of unwinding, partner relationship breaking, data corruption | Immediately — same hour |
| HIGH | Blocking multiple transactions, systematic bug, timing deadline at risk | Same day |
| Medium | Single transaction issue, process question, payment dispute | Within 1-2 business days |
| Low | Documentation gap, feature request, non-urgent improvement | This week or backlog |

---

## Escalation Principles

1. **Try to resolve at the lowest level first** — check tracker, check CITY, check Ramp before escalating
2. **Slack DMs move faster than channel posts** for urgent issues
3. **Document as you go** — the escalation path should leave a paper trail
4. **Escalate early on timing issues** — a ≤7-day assignment window can't be recovered if you wait
5. **Name the DRI** — if a resolution is unclear, ask explicitly who owns it
