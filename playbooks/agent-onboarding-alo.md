# Playbook: Agent Onboarding — ALO

**Trigger:** New agent/broker approved for the Agent Led Offers program.

---

## Step 1 — Send Welcome Email
Use the welcome email template: [../templates/agent-welcome-email.md](../templates/agent-welcome-email.md)

Customize for:
- Agent's name and brokerage
- Market (determines Cash vs. Cash Plus content)

## Step 2 — CC Training Coordinator
Always CC Bryson Taggart on the welcome email.
This triggers training scheduling automatically — don't skip this.

## Step 3 — Share Key Resources in the Email
Include:
- Key Agent app access instructions
- How to request an assessment (Diligence Visit)
- How to finalize an offer
- Cash Plus overview (if applicable to their market)

## Step 4 — HubSpot Record (if CP ELA)
If the agent is a non-partner Cash Plus ELA:
- Add to HubSpot contacts
- Create or link their company record
- Apply appropriate tags for program tracking

## Step 5 — Add to Payment Tracker
Add the broker to "Agent Led Offers Payments 2026" (Google Drive) so future closings can be tracked and paid correctly.

---

## Market-Specific Notes

| Market Type | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| Cash Plus market | Include CP overview in welcome materials |
| Cash-only market | Standard welcome, no CP section |
| Expansion market | Confirm market is live before onboarding |

---

## After Onboarding

- Confirm agent has logged into Key Agent app
- Answer any questions that come back from welcome email
- Track in ALO payments sheet when their first closing occurs
