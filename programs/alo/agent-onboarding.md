# ALO — Agent Onboarding Guide

**Trigger:** New agent approved for the Agent Led Offers program.

---

## Onboarding Steps

### Step 1 — Send Welcome Email
Use the welcome email template from [../../templates/agent-welcome-email.md](../../templates/agent-welcome-email.md).

Key customization:
- Agent's name
- Market (determines whether to include Cash or Cash Plus information)
- Training coordinator CC (Bryson Taggart)

### Step 2 — CC Training Coordinator
Always CC Bryson Taggart on the welcome email so training can be scheduled automatically.

### Step 3 — Share Key Resources
Include in the welcome email:
- Key Agent app access instructions
- How to request and submit an assessment (Diligence Visit)
- How to finalize an offer in the system
- Cash Plus information (if applicable to their market)

### Step 4 — HubSpot Record
If the agent is a non-partner Cash Plus ELA:
- Add to HubSpot contacts
- Create or link their company record
- Tag appropriately for tracking

### Step 5 — Track in Payment Sheet
Add the agent/broker to the "Agent Led Offers Payments 2026" tracking sheet so future payments can be processed correctly.

## Market-Specific Notes

- **Cash Plus markets:** Include CP product overview in welcome materials
- **Cash-only markets:** Standard welcome — no CP section needed
- **Expansion markets:** Confirm market is live before onboarding agent
