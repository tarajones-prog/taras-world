# Agent Led Offers (ALO) — Program Overview

**Status:** Active — Nationwide
**Type:** Partner agent acquisition program
**Last updated:** 2026-03

---

## What Is ALO?

Agent Led Offers is a program where partner agents submit offers on Opendoor properties on behalf of buyers. Partner brokers receive a payment per successful closing as an incentive for participation.

The program was launched nationally in 2025 and is now in steady-state operations.

---

## Payment Structure

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Payment amount | Per successful closing (standard rate) |
| Paid to | Partner broker |
| Payment memo | "This payment relates to our Agent Led Offers pilot. We are compensating partner brokers when a referred seller sells to Opendoor." |
| Payment vehicle | Ramp PO |

## Key Lessons from Launch

> **Document processes early, even before they're needed at scale.** The nationwide ALO launch went well because operational detail was owned from the pilot phase forward.

> **Build for handoff from the start.** Shared trackers, clear payment memos, and Ramp POs make transitions smooth. Manual tracking doesn't scale.
