# HubSpot — CRM Guide

**What:** HubSpot is the CRM used for tracking agent contacts, partner companies, and communications across the Agent Partner Programs team.

---

## How We Use HubSpot

### Agent Records
- New ALO agents → add as contacts
- Link to their brokerage company record
- Tag with relevant program(s) (ALO, Cash Plus ELA, etc.)
- Track communication history

### Company Records
- Each brokerage = a company record
- Link all agent contacts from that brokerage to the company
- Useful for rolling up activity by partner firm

### Non-Partner Cash Plus ELAs
When an agent is a non-partner Cash Plus ELA (not formally enrolled but acting as ELA):
- Add to HubSpot contacts
- Create or link a company record for their brokerage
- Ask Steve if unclear whether to create a company — open question as of early 2026
